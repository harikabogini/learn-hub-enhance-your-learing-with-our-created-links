Best Books for JEE Mains and Advanced Preparation

Physics

  • Concepts of Physics by H.C. Verma - Volume 1 and 2
  • Problems in General Physics by I.E. Irodov
  • Fundamentals of Physics by Halliday, Resnick, and Walker
  • Understanding Physics Series by D.C. Pandey (Arihant Publications)

Chemistry

Physical Chemistry

  • Physical Chemistry by P.W. Atkins
  • Numerical Chemistry by P. Bahadur
  • Physical Chemistry by O.P. Tandon

Inorganic Chemistry

  • Concise Inorganic Chemistry by J.D. Lee
  • Inorganic Chemistry by O.P. Tandon

Organic Chemistry

  • Organic Chemistry by Morrison and Boyd
  • Organic Chemistry by O.P. Tandon
  • Advanced Problems in Organic Chemistry by M.S. Chauhan

Mathematics

  • IIT Mathematics by M.L. Khanna
  • Mathematics for Class 11 & 12 by R.D. Sharma
  • Problems in Calculus of One Variable by I.A. Maron
  • Higher Algebra by Hall and Knight
  • Trigonometry by S.L. Loney
  • Coordinate Geometry by S.L. Loney
  • Problems in Mathematics by Vikas Gupta (Balaji Publications)
